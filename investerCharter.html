Investor Charter

Your rights, our commitment. Understanding the framework that protects your investments and guides our responsibilities as a trusted financial partner.

Home Investor Charter
SECTION A

Vision and Mission Statements

  • Vision: Invest with knowledge and safety.
  • Mission: Every investor should be able to invest in right investment products based on their needs, manage and monitor them to meet their goals, access reports and enjoy financial wellness.
SECTION B

Details of Business Transacted

  • Publish research reports based on thorough research activities
  • Provide independent and unbiased views on securities
  • Offer recommendations disclosing financial interests
  • Conduct research based on publicly available information
  • Conduct annual audits for compliance
  • Ensure advertisements adhere to SEBI guidelines
  • Maintain detailed records of all client interactions
SECTION C

Details of Services Provided to Investors

Onboarding of Clients

  • Sharing comprehensive terms and conditions of research services
  • Completing KYC for all fee-paying clients

Disclosure to Clients

  • Disclose material information for informed decision-making, including business activities, disciplinary history, and terms of service
  • Disclose extent of use of Artificial Intelligence tools in research services
  • Disclose conflicts of interest when distributing third-party research reports
  • Disclose any conflicts between research services and other activities

Commitments to Clients

  • Distribute research reports without discrimination
  • Maintain confidentiality until public disclosure
  • Respect data privacy and protect confidential information
  • Disclose and adhere to service timelines
  • Provide clear guidance for complex and high-risk products
  • Treat all clients with honesty and integrity
SECTION D

Grievance Redressal Mechanism

Filing Complaints with Research Analyst

For any grievance, approach the concerned Research Analyst who shall strive to redress the complaint within 21 days of receipt.

Filing on SCORES or with RAASB

SCORES 2.0

Web-based centralized grievance redressal system of SEBI

https://scores.sebi.gov.in
  • Two-level review process: First review by designated body (RAASB), followed by second review by SEBI
  • Email to designated email ID of RAASB for complaints

SMARTODR Platform

If not satisfied with resolution provided, file complaints on SMARTODR platform for online conciliation or arbitration.

SECTION F

Expectations from Investors

As an informed investor, certain responsibilities help ensure a smooth and secure investment experience.

Do's

  • Always deal with SEBI registered Research Analysts
  • Verify valid registration certificate
  • Check SEBI registration number on official website
  • Pay attention to disclosures in research reports
  • Make payments through banking channels only
  • Ask all relevant questions before acting on recommendations
  • Seek clarifications on complex and high-risk products
  • Be aware of your right to exit services
  • Provide feedback on services received
  • Maintain duly signed receipts of all payments

Don'ts

  • Don't fall for assured or guaranteed returns
  • Don't provide funds for investment to Research Analyst
  • Don't fall prey to market rumors or unverified tips
  • Don't get attracted to limited period discount offers
  • Don't share login credentials or passwords
  • Don't ignore regulatory warnings and alerts
  • Don't engage with unregistered advisors
  • Don't make hasty investment decisions under pressure
