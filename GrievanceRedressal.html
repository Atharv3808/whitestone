SEBI SCORES

SEBI Complaints Redressal System (SCORES) is an online platform that makes it easy for investors to retrieve and track their complaints. If an investor is not satisfied with the steps taken by a Research Analyst, they can lodge a complaint through SCORES. The Research Analyst will resolve complaints on a best effort basis within 21 calendar days of receipt.
