Grievance Redressal & Dispute Settlement

We are committed to resolving your concerns promptly and fairly. Your trust is our priority, and we ensure every grievance receives the attention it deserves.

Our Commitment to You

At Whitestone Capital, we prioritize your satisfaction and peace of mind. As part of our robust client grievance redressal and dispute settlement mechanism, we are dedicated to resolving all disputes between clients and Research Analysts through prompt, amicable negotiations.

We believe in transparency, accountability, and swift resolution. If you have any grievances or complaints regarding our services, we encourage you to reach out to our Compliance Officer, who will ensure your concerns are addressed efficiently and professionally.

SEBI SCORES

SEBI Complaints Redressal System (SCORES) is an online platform designed to streamline the complaint process for investors.

Through SCORES, you can easily lodge, retrieve, and track your complaints in real-time. If you are not satisfied with the resolution provided by a Research Analyst, you have the option to escalate your concern through this secure and efficient platform.

Our commitment is to resolve all complaints on a best-effort basis within 21 calendar days of receipt.

Visit SCORES Website

Online Dispute Resolution

If you remain unsatisfied with the resolution provided by a Market Participant, you can escalate your complaint to the SMARTODR platform.

SMARTODR offers online conciliation and arbitration services, providing an efficient and impartial mechanism for resolving disputes. This platform ensures that your concerns are addressed through a fair and transparent process.

We support the use of ODR services and are committed to cooperating fully to achieve a satisfactory resolution for all parties involved.
