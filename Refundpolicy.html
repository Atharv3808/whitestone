Refund Policy

Understanding our refund terms and conditions

We prioritise our customers and are dedicated to delivering the highest quality services. It is crucial for our clients to understand that, in accordance with SEBI guidelines, we cannot provide a return assurance or guarantee on our research calls or recommendations.

Refunds, if applicable, will only be processed on a pro-rata basis of the subscription fee, irrespective of the client's outcomes. Once a service is subscribed to and payment is completed, it can only be cancelled or refunded on a pro-rata basis.

Client Satisfaction

If a client is dissatisfied for any unforeseen reason, they may contact us for guidance and support. We will make every effort to enhance client satisfaction in such cases. However, any request for cancellation or refund will strictly be processed on a pro-rata basis only and completed within 7 to 10 working days from the date of approval.

Important Considerations

Before making any payment, clients are strongly encouraged to review all details of our services and the support we provide. They should also familiarise themselves with our Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Refund Policy. It is essential to understand that refunds are only possible on a pro-rata basis, as per SEBI notifications, circulars, and laws.

Acknowledge that there is no avenue for claiming trading or investment losses based on the recommendations in the research report.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be credited within 7 to 10 days in the original payment mode.
